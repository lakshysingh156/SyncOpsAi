CORE PRODUCT VISION
===================

SyncOps AI is NOT a CRUD dashboard.

SyncOps AI is an AI-Native Observability Platform.

The final product should answer:

* Why did my deployment fail?
* Which service caused the incident?
* Which logs correlate with the issue?
* Which metrics degraded?
* What is the probable root cause?
* What changed recently?
* What should I do next?

The platform should feel like a modern DevOps/SRE command center.
